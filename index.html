Q1. What are the general prerequisites for immigration ?
Prerequisites vary by country and visa type but typically include a valid passport, financial stability, health and character certificates, and, for certain categories, proof of employment or admission to an educational institution.​

Q3. What IELTS score is required for Canada?
For Canada, the required IELTS score depends on the visa category. For example, the Federal Skilled Worker Program typically requires a minimum of CLB 7, which corresponds to an IELTS score of 6.0 in each band.​
Q4. How much time is needed for visa renewal?
Visa renewal processing times vary by country and visa type. Generally, it can take anywhere from a few days to several weeks. It's advisable to apply well in advance of your visa's expiration date.​
